VR Medical Set

A VR Medical Set is a virtual reality system used in healthcare for training, simulations, rehabilitation, and pain management, enhancing medical education and patient care.

Aerosol

An aerosol is a medical device that delivers medication in mist form for inhalation, commonly used for respiratory conditions like asthma and COPD, or for emergencies.

Brain chip

A brain chip is an implantable neural device that interfaces with the brain to restore functions, treat neurological disorders, or enhance cognitive abilities.
