Artificial Finger

A robotic finger is a prosthetic or assistive device that restores or enhances finger movement using advanced sensors and motorized control.

Artificial Hand

A robotic hand is a prosthetic or bionic device that replicates hand movements, aiding amputees or enhancing dexterity through advanced sensors and neural control.

Robotic Arm

A human robot arm is a bionic or prosthetic limb that mimics natural arm movements, aiding amputees or patients with mobility impairments
