Artificial Leg

A robotic leg is a prosthetic or exoskeletal device that aids mobility, using advanced sensors and motors to mimic natural leg movements for amputees

Artificial Foot

A robotic foot is a prosthetic device that mimics natural foot movement, enhancing balance and mobility for amputees through advanced sensors and motorized control.

Foot orthosis

A robotic orthosis is a wearable assistive device that supports or enhances limb movement using motors and sensors, aiding rehabilitation and mobility
